IMMEDIATE ACTIONS ON SUSPICION:
1. Stop all bird movement on and off premises immediately
2. Call District Livestock Development Officer (DLD): 02-653-4444
3. Isolate sick birds from healthy birds
4. Do NOT allow visitors or vehicles into/out of farm
5. Wear gloves, mask, and protective clothing before entering affected area
6. Do NOT consume or sell sick or dead birds

OFFICIAL RESPONSE (DLD-led):
- Quarantine radius: 3 km protection zone, 10 km surveillance zone
- Culling: humane depopulation of all poultry on affected premises
- Stamping out: destroy eggs, feed, litter, and equipment
- Dead bird disposal: incineration or deep burial (>1 m depth)
- Decontamination: sodium hydroxide 2% or glutaraldehyde across all surfaces
- Full cleaning + disinfection sequence mandatory before restocking

MOVEMENT RESTRICTIONS:
- No movement of live poultry, eggs, or used equipment within zones
- Vehicle disinfection checkpoints at zone boundaries
- Fresh poultry products may move only with official veterinary certificate

ZOONOTIC PRECAUTIONS:
- H5N1 CFR in humans ~57%. Wear N95 + gloves + goggles + coverall during culling
- Post-exposure antiviral: oseltamivir (Tamiflu) 75mg twice daily x 5 days
- Report any fever/respiratory symptoms within 10 days of exposure to public health
- Healthcare providers: isolate suspected human H5N1 cases, airborne precautions

VACCINATION STRATEGY (where permitted):
- Emergency vaccination with matching H5 clade vaccine may be authorized
- Vaccinated flocks must be differentiated from infected flocks (DIVA strategy)
- Vaccination does not replace culling in HPAI outbreaks

SURVEILLANCE AFTER OUTBREAK:
- Minimum 28-day standstill after last death + full decontamination
- Sentinel bird testing before restocking
- Enhanced surveillance for 90 days post-restocking
- Report all-clear to DLD before resuming commercial operations

BIOSECURITY UPGRADES (permanent):
- All-in/all-out production system
- Strict visitor log and disinfection footbath at every entrance
- No sharing of equipment between farms
- Wild bird exclusion: cover ponds, repair shed gaps
- Rodent control program (weekly)
- Dead bird disposal protocol with log records
